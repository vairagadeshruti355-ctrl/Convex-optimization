# Convex-optimization

Introduction:<br>
<br>
The motive behind doing this project is to learn the convex optimization and focus on its real world modelling problems.
Solving problems computationally using CVXPY.  The emphasis is on understanding optimization variables, objectives, constraints, and practical implementations rather than mathematical proofs.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
Topics Studied:<br>
<br>
<br>
Week 1:
Optimization problems , convex sets , convex functions , basic constrained optimization , introduction to CVXPY , solving least square problems, understanding its geometric intuition, and being able to recognize how convex optimization problems look like.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
Week 2:
Studying convexity rules, linear programming , Qudratic programming , optimization modelling, constructing better understanding about the variaables converting real problems into convex optimization problems, understanding constraints and maximizing and minimizing, implementing different models in CVXPY<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
Week 3:
Understanding commom convex functions, Norms, Quadratics, composition rules, understanding why CVXPY accepts/rejects expression, experimenting with CVXPY expressions, learning disciplined convex programming.<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>
Week 4:
Understanding how optimization appears in machine learning and scientific modelling, studied topics like least squares, ridge regression, sparse regression(LASSO) and regularization.<br>
